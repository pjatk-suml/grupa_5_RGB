Color classification project - ML implementation

SUML gr5 - POC

Welcome to our project. You can interact with it in one of two ways:
- You can try out our model and see how it does:
-> you will be presented with a page where you can put R, G and B values
-> our model will perform classification and return you one of the colors, which is closest to given by you.


- You can also help to relearn our model:
-> you will be presented with a page and a rectangle of a random color
-> you will need to choose between predefined colors, which one of them describes the color displayed the best

Color Classification Project - ML Implementation

SUML gr5 - POC

Welcome to our project! You can interact with it in one of two ways:

You can try our model and see how it works:

> A page will be displayed where you can put the R , G and B values

> Our model will classify and return one of the colors closest to the color given by you.

You can also help re-learn our model:

> A randomly colored page and a rectangle will appear

> You have to choose from the predefined colors which one best describes the displayed color
